Tennis Facts:

  • Tennis was originated in France in the 12th century. Tennis was originally played with hands instead of a racket.
  • There are 4 major Tennis tournaments. Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and the US Open.
  • In tennis, the scoring system is unlike any other sport. The scores are 15, 30, 40, and then game. If both players reach 40, it is called deuce. A player must win two consecutive points to win the game
  • The longest tennis match was 11 hours and 5 minutes.

Health Benefits:

  • Cardiovascular Health: Tennis gets your heart rate up which can help decrease the risk of heart disease.
  • Muscle Strength: Tennis helps build muscles in core parts of a body.
  • Weight: Tennis helps people burn calories and lose/control your weight.
  • Mental Health: Tennis helps improve concentration, reducing stress, and improve problem solving skills.
  • Hand-Eye Coordination: In tennis, people need to have great timing and coordination between hands and eyes.
